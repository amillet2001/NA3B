Shoto Todoroki is the tritagonist of the anime and manga franchise, 
My Hero Academia. He is a student at U.A. High School training to become a Pro Hero. 
He got into U.A. through official recommendations. He is also Midoriya's second-best friend and friendly rival. 
He is the youngest son of Endeavor and the younger brother of both Fuyumi and Natsuo Todoroki as well his eldest brother, Toya Todoroki.
